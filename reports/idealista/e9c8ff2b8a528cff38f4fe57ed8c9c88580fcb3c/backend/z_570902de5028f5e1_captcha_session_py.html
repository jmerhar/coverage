11class CaptchaSession(Base): 

12 """Short-lived session for manual captcha solving via Telegram. 

13 

14 When the scraper encounters a CAPTCHA it cannot auto-resolve, it creates 

15 a CaptchaSession row with the captcha URL and a random token. A Telegram 

16 message is sent with a link to a solve page (keyed by token). Once the 

17 user solves the captcha and submits the datadome cookie, the scraper polls 

18 for cookie_value and injects it into the browser. 

19 

20 Every session also carries a token-keyed "resume now" link that lets the 

21 operator end the scraper's cooldown early (a global "resume scraping" signal — 

22 see the resume endpoint and ``services.scraper_control``). ``target_label`` is 

23 kept only for display on the solve/resume pages. 

24 """ 

25 

26 __tablename__ = "captcha_sessions" 

27
